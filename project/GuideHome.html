Why It Matters

Cybersecurity isn’t just for large corporations or IT professionals. It affects everyone—whether you're checking email, shopping online, or using cloud services. Hackers don’t discriminate. In fact, individuals are often the easiest targets because they don’t expect to be attacked.

From identity theft to data loss, the risks of ignoring basic online protection are real. A single weak password or unpatched device can open the door to financial loss, privacy invasion, or even reputation damage.

Understanding how to protect yourself empowers you to safely navigate the digital world, protect those around you, and avoid becoming a victim of cybercrime. When you're informed, you're in control.
